# Introduction

Welcome to Celerix Dev Toolbox, your ultimate companion for streamlining your development workflow. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting out, this toolbox has got you covered with a suite of powerful tools designed to enhance your productivity and efficiency.

Key Features:
- **Interactive Dashboard**: A widget-driven interface for up-to-date information.
- **Kanban Board**: A visual tool for managing tasks and projects.
- **Terminal**: A full-featured terminal emulator for executing commands on your local machine.

Key Mini-tools:
- **Base64 Encoder/Decoder**: Convert data between text and base64 using Base64 encoding and decoding.
- **UUID Generator**: Generate universally unique identifiers (UUIDs) for your projects.
- **JWT Inspector**: Validate and decode JSON Web Tokens (JWTs) for secure authentication.
